RandoBot

Description: RIVALS is an intense first-person shooter game on Roblox, developed by Nosniy Games. Players engage in competitive duels ranging from 1v1 to 5v5, aiming to be the first to win five rounds by eliminating opponents. The game offers various modes, including Team Deathmatch and Free For All, accessible through the lobby's green play button. In the lobby, players can access shops, leaderboards, and a shooting range to test weapons. Earning keys by completing tasks allows players to unlock new weapons and cosmetics. Each weapon has specific contracts that, when fulfilled, grant additional rewards. The game is available on multiple platforms, including Desktop, Phones, Tablets, XBOX, and PlayStation 5.
